English

Nine Steeps does not collect, transmit, or share any user data.

All data — your tea library, brewing sessions, and tasting notes — is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. The app does not connect to any remote servers, does not include analytics or advertising SDKs, and does not require an account or login.

When you delete the app, all data stored by Nine Steeps is removed from your device.

On-device system services used:

  • UserNotifications — for timer reminders (if you grant permission).
  • ActivityKit — for the Live Activity that appears on your Lock Screen and (on watchOS 11+) the Apple Watch Smart Stack while a brewing session is active.
  • WatchConnectivity — for syncing the active brewing session between your iPhone and paired Apple Watch.
  • App Group shared container — for sharing the local SwiftData store between the iPhone app, Apple Watch app, and Live Activity widget extension. No data leaves the device.

All of these services run entirely on your device. Nine Steeps does not send any data over the network.

Nine Steeps does not include any third-party SDKs, analytics, advertising, attribution, or crash-reporting libraries.

Nine Steeps is intended for adult tea drinkers and is not directed at children under 13.

If this policy ever changes, the updated policy will appear here and in the app's Settings → Privacy screen before the change takes effect.

Contact: support@rizkcorsight.com
