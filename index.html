Purchases

Nine Steeps is free to download with 3 days of full access. Starting that 3-day access window happens locally on your device and does not create a subscription or charge. That purchase or restore action is handled entirely by Apple’s App Store (iOS) or Google Play (Android) through their own in-app purchase systems. Nine Steeps never sees or receives your name, payment card, or billing details. The developer receives only anonymous, aggregate sales figures from Apple and Google — never anything tied to you. Apple’s and Google’s handling of the transaction is governed by their own privacy policies.

Nine Steeps - Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 27, 2026

Store Listing Alignment

Checked June 30, 2026. This page was compared with the public App Store and Google Play listings. Where a store privacy or data-safety label was visible, it said the developer does not collect user data.

Accurate app behavior: Tea profiles, timers, tasting notes, cellar entries, reminders, watch/widget state, and settings stay local. The 3-day trial starts locally; Apple App Store or Google Play handles only the optional full-access purchase, restore, refund, receipt, or price display. No accounts, ads, analytics, tracking, or developer backend are used.

Nine Steeps is a local-first tea timer and tasting journal. We do not sell personal data, run ads, use analytics or tracking, or require an account.

Scope

This policy covers Nine Steeps on Android, iPhone, iPad, and Apple Watch.

Data Stored By The App

Nine Steeps can store tea profiles, brew sessions, tasting notes, cellar inventory entries, app settings, and Full Access purchase status. This data is stored locally on your device.

  • On Android, app data is stored in Android app-private storage and private SharedPreferences. Android cloud backup is disabled for Nine Steeps.
  • On Apple platforms, app data is stored locally with Apple's SwiftData framework and related app containers used by the iPhone app, Apple Watch app, and widgets.
  • On Apple platforms, the local preview's first-seen and last-seen dates are stored in Apple Keychain. These dates may survive app deletion and reinstallation on the same device or Apple Account; they contain no tea library, tasting note, account, or payment data.
  • Apple may include app data and photos in device or iCloud backups unless you disable those backups. Nine Steeps does not operate or receive those backups.

Purchases

Nine Steeps starts with a 3-day full-access trial. After the trial, continued full access is available through one in-app purchase. Android purchases are processed by Google Play Billing. Apple purchases are processed by Apple StoreKit.

The app may store purchase status so full access stays available after purchase. We do not receive or store your full payment card details.

Exports And Sharing

If you choose to export your library or tasting archive, Android opens the system share sheet and Apple platforms use the system sharing flow. The destination you choose, such as a notes app, email app, cloud drive, or messaging app, handles that exported copy under its own privacy practices.

Permissions And System Services

  • Android vibration permission is used only for timer feedback.
  • Android billing permission is used only for purchase and restore flows.
  • Apple UserNotifications may be used for timer reminders if you grant permission.
  • Apple ActivityKit may be used for Live Activities during active brewing sessions.
  • Apple WatchConnectivity may be used to sync the active brewing session between iPhone and a paired Apple Watch.

Nine Steeps does not request location, contacts, camera, microphone, or advertising ID access.

Third Parties

Nine Steeps does not include advertising, analytics, attribution, tracking, or crash reporting. Payment processing is handled by Google Play or Apple StoreKit when you choose to make a purchase.

Deleting Data

Deleting the app removes its installed app container from that device. Copies can remain in Apple device or iCloud backups, and exported copies remain wherever you saved or shared them. The local preview dates stored in Apple Keychain may survive deletion and reinstallation on the same device or Apple Account. Marketplace purchase history is maintained by Apple or Google. Nine Steeps does not receive or store any of this data on Nine Steeps servers.

Children

Nine Steeps is intended for tea drinkers and is not directed to children under 13.
